Introduction

    • Hyperlinks, or links, are fundamental to the web. They connect various resources, 
        allowing users to navigate from one web page to another, 
        download files, or jump to specific sections within a page.
    • The < a> tag, short for "anchor," is one of the most fundamental/powerful elements in HTML.
    • It is used to create hyperlinks, which are essential for navigating between web pages, 
        linking to external sites, or jumping to specific parts of a page.
